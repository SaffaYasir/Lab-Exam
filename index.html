Our Mission

      

The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character,
a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning. The University shall stand committed to creating an environment conducive
for attracting the best students, faculty and supporting staff for contributing to the development of a prosperous, peaceful
and enlightened society.Air University aspires to be among the leading national universities, excelling in teaching,
learning, research, innovation and public service.

      

AU Faculties and Institute

      

Air University was established by the Pakistan Air Force (PAF) in 2002 to meet the growing educational and technological needs of the PAF and the country.
It was granted the status of a public university.The university has been involved in collaborations with international institutions and organizations
to enhance the quality of education and research.the university remains under the management of PAF's education command. The university
has five faculties offering both undergraduate and graduate courses.

AU Campuses

      

Islamabad Campus

Established in 2002, The campus is located near the Margalla foothills in the south east corner of sector E-9 (PAF Complex) of Islamabad. The University is a degree awarding autonomous institution and its graduates are offered good placement opportunities in the job market.

Aerospace and Aviation Campus Kamra

Established in 2017, The campus is established in close vacinity of Pakistan Aeronautical Complex Attock and will have linkeges with Aviation Research Inovation and Development (AVRID). Aviation Design Institution (AVDI) and National Aerospace Science and Technology Park (NASTP) .

Multan Campus

Established in 2011, Air University established its second campus in Multan, Punjab, Pakistan. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in management sciences, accounting and finance, information technology, mathematics and computer science.

Kharian Campus

Established in 2023, ir University has established its new campus at Kharian which will be fully functional from Spring 2024 Semester. It will initially offer undergraduate programs followed by graduate programs in different disciplines.
